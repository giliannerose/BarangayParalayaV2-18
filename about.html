About Barangay Paralaya

Barangay Paralaya is one of the thriving communities in the municipality, known for its active citizen participation, commitment to peace and order, and dedication to sustainable local development. The barangay continuously works to promote the welfare of its residents through effective governance and community-driven programs.

Vision and Mission

Vision

To build a safe, united, and progressive community that values discipline, inclusivity, and cooperation where every resident enjoys a high quality of life and equal opportunity.


Mission

To deliver efficient and transparent public service, promote environmental sustainability, ensure peace and order, and empower citizens through active participation in barangay initiatives and programs.

Barangay Goals

  • Strengthen disaster preparedness and community resilience.
  • Enhance public safety and peacekeeping programs.
  • Promote cleanliness, health, and environmental awareness.
  • Support livelihood and youth development activities.
  • Encourage transparency and accountability in governance.
